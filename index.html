About Me

Andrew Damico is a full-stack software developer that currently works at Northwestern University. He works with many programming languages such as: Java, Oracle 11g PL/SQL, MySQL, Oracle ADF, PHP. He has earned a Bachelors in Computer Science in 2014. Currently, he is attending Northwestern's coding boot camp.

In his spare time, Andrew enjoys hiking, camping, volunteering for organizations such as Habitat for Humanity, playing online computer games, and listening to music. Andrew, along with his identical twin brother, has earned the rank of Eagle Scout. He has 3 brothers and 2 dogs.

While in high school, Andrew has taken a computer repair class and a networking class. Over the past 16 years, Andrew has repaired many laptops and built 9 desktops. His latest desktop has 2 water cooling systems and is powerful enough to even function as a small server.
